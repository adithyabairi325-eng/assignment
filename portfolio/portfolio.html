About Me

My academic journey began with a fascination for how code can be transformed into real-world solutions. Every lecture and lab session fuels my curiosity to bridge theory with practical applications. I see programming not just as syntax and logic, but as a powerful tool to solve human problems. C and Python are my primary languages of exploration, and I enjoy experimenting with their versatility. Optimization is not just about speed, but also about clarity and maintainability. As an aspiring AI enthusiast, I constantly seek opportunities to apply algorithms to practical builds. Machine learning excites me because it allows systems to learn and adapt like humans.
